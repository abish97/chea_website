CheA and IIChe

The Chemical Engineering Association (ChEA), established in (year), aims at promoting awareness and knowledge about chemical engineering to outside masses as well as provides a forum for mutual interaction to students, faculty, both past and present, of the Department of Chemical Engineering, NIT Warangal. The association ultimately aims at enabling the students and faculty to foster an interactive and intellectual environment.



IIche Student Chapter:


IIChE (Indian Institute of chemical engineers) student chapter was formed in (year), to represent department of Chemical engineering, NIT Warangal, at various local and national events held by IIChE. The main aim of the student chapter is to provide students with a platform to showcase their skills and talent at national level.



ChEA and IIChE together have conducted many events to help students enhance their interpersonal as well as technical skills. These events include Know your branch, Intern Connect, interactive with professors, guest lectures, mock GD sessions and Learn in legion, the first of its kind, helping students to ace in group discussions, interviews in internships and placements.



Both the associations share a common goal of striving for development of chemical engineering department and student fraternity.
