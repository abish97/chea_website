Placement Talk

“Placement Talk” was conducted on the 22nd of March at the Chemical department seminar hall. It began at 5pm and was concluded at 8:30 pm. The final years spoke in detail about their application and interview procedure. Advice regarding preparation was given and emphasis was put on the importance of being thorough with basics of certain subjects. The event was attended majorly by 3rd years along with a few 2nd years. The GATE rank achiever (AIR 44) had also provided definitive advice and explained her preparation techniques for obtaining a successful top rank for the future GATE aspirants. Placement Talk thereby succeeded in bridging the gap between the final years and the younger generations with a free-flow of their highly valuable wisdom and experience. This information amassed, will surely raise the quality of students sitting for placements next year, up a notch!
